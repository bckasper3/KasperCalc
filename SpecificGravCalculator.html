DENSITY CONVERSIONS TO S.G.
Kg/m³ ⮕ S.G.


Plain Text Formula:(Density of Liquid (kg/m³)/Density of Water (kg/m³)) = Specific Gravity of Liquid
CodeCogs/LaTex:\[\frac{Density\,of\,Liquid(\frac{kg}{m^{3}})}{Density\,\:of\,Water(\frac{kg}{m^{3}})}=S.G.\]
Lb/Gallon ⮕ S.G.


Plain Text Formula:Density of Liquid (lb/gallon)*(1 Gallon/231 (in³))*(27679.90471(kg/m³)/1 (lb/in³))*(1/Density of Water (kg/m³)) = Specific Gravity of Liquid
CodeCogs/LaTex:\[Density\,of\,Liquid(\frac{lb}{gallon})*\frac{1\,Gallon}{231\,in^{3}}*\frac{(27679.90471\,\frac{kg}{m^{3}})}{(\frac{lb}{in^{3}})}*\frac{1}{Density\,\:of\,Water(\frac{kg}{m^{3}})}=S.G.\]
Lb/in³ ⮕ S.G


Plain Text Formula:Density of Liquid (lb/in³)*(27679.90471(kg/m³)/1 (lb/in³))*(1/Density of Water (kg/m³)) = Specific Gravity of Liquid
CodeCogs/LaTex: \[Density\,of\,Liquid(\frac{lb}{in^{3}})*\frac{(27679.90471\,\frac{kg}{m^{3}})}{(\frac{lb}{in^{3}})}*\frac{1}{Density\,\:of\,Water(\frac{kg}{m^{3}})}=S.G.\]
